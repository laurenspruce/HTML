Tabby Cats

Purpose

Welcome to the very first page of the cat breeds website. The purpose of this website is to educate more people on the various different breeds, and ultimately make a better decision when purchasing a cat, or looking after different breeds.

Why should I learn this information?

This is so that you can make a better decision when purchasing a cat.

Here are a list of reasons ranked from most important:

  1. Better quality of life for your kitten/cat
  2. Save money and resources from vet trips
  3. Overall better lifestyle for you and your cat
  4. Educate other cat owners
  5. Perhaps save a cats life!
